Class Options

If there is a specific curriculum you would like to follow, please contact me.

Free Talk & Homework Help

This class is tailored to each student and helps build confidence in English skills. Class can range from a variety of questions about the student's daily life, interests, and dreams to looking at the student's English homework and assisting with any difficulties.

Phonics Focus

Let's make reading fun! One of the most challenging aspects of English is the spelling, which is often irregular and full of inconsistencies. In this class, we will make sure to master the keys to successful reading.

Reading Class

Intended for intermediate and advanced students. For intermediate students, we will read fun short stories from all over the world. For advanced students, we will do close readings followed by critical discussion, including literary styles and historical context.

Writing Class

Intended for intermediate and advanced students. Many times, students who have strong speaking or reading skills struggle with writing grammatically-correct and dynamic sentences and essays. In this class, we will practice how to express oneself well in written form.

ABOUT ME

Andrea Camille Hull

Hello! I look forward to getting to know your child and helping them on their English journey.

I received a Master's degree from the University of California, Los Angeles, a Bachelor of Arts fron Bryn Mawr College, and I was a Children's Librarian at a busy suburban public library before becoming a mother. I have tutored and taught English on-and-off for many years, both in the United States and in other countries. For the past two years, my family has been living in Japan in a small town north of Kyoto.

Growing up, we spoke two languages in the home - English and Spanish - and as an adult, I studies a few more languages, including Russian and Italian. I believe strongly in the importance of being multilingual! I know what it is like to learn a new language and also how to encourage language learning.

Learning should always be engaging and fun in order to keep a student's attention and ensure the maximum amount of learning. It is very important that my students feel comfortable practicing their grammar and vocabulary, and that they are not afraid to ask questions or make mistakes. I truly love teaching and am constantly inspired by the vivacity and creativity of my students!

Policies

Policies & Procedures

If the student needs to cancel class, please do so within 12 hours of class time. If either the student or the teacher experiences technical problems that cannot be resolved, class will be cancelled and rescheduled at no cost. The teacher will remain in class for the full 25-minute class time. If the student is late, only the remaining time will be used to teach.
Cancellations
All classes for the month must be paid in full at the beginning of each month. The parent will receive an invoice from the teacher by e-mail and can pay using a credit card, PayPal, or WeChat Pay. A small processing surcharge of 3% will be added to the total cost.
Payment
Class will be held via ClassIn. If possible, please have the student use ClassIn on a desktop or laptop computer, as there are more limitations when using ClassIn on a tablet. Zoom, Skype, and Voov are also a possibility if ClassIn cannot be used by the student.
